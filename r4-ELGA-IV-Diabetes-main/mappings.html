Mapping from Diabetes to IPS

On this page you can find the mapping from the data model based on the “Rahmenkonzept Integrierte Versorgung Diabetes mellitus Typ 2” to the data model based on the International Patient Summary (IPS).

Note, that only the relevant modules of the IPS will be included. Furthermore, even if not all modules of the IPS are mentioned in the mappings it is of course allowed to use them, if applicable.
