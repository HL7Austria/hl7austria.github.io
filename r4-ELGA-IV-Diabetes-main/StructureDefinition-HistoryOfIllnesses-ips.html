Logical Model: Past History Of Illnesses (IPS)

Official URL: https://diab.iv.elga.gv.at/StructureDefinition/HistoryOfIllnesses-ips Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-05-27 Computable Name: HistoryOfIllnessesIps

Abbildung der Datenfelder, die für den Entwurf der Datenspezifikation des Rahmenkonzepts Integrierte Versorgung Diabetes Mellitus Typ 2 erforderlich sind, auf dem des IPS-Modul “Past History Of Illnesses”.

Refer to the mapping from the logical model of diabetes to the logical model based on the International Patient Summary (IPS) in order to get an idea how the IPS relates to diabetes.

Usage:

  • This Logical Model Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide
